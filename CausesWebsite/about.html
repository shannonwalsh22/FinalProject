ABOUT THE EVENT

OUR STORY

NYAWCs fabric is made of and created by the NYC pan Asian communities we serve. Food and drink are unifying forces of our cultures.

COLLECTIVE EFFORT

Gathering supporters, new and old, NYAC will host its inaugural night market to celebrate our collective support of violence free lives, honor the journeys of our survivors, and uplift our community with a festive gathering around food.

EVENT FEATURES

Designed as a two part event-- a special chef tasting hour, followed by an indoor night market after party, we will engage the talents of a pan Asian grouping of NYC based chefs and restauarnts.
